What is money shadow work?
🌹

The Money Shadow

Our relationship with money is shaped by what we were taught, witnessed, and told — often before we had words for it. Shame, fear, and unworthiness live quietly, driving our financial behaviour.

💛

Money as Mirror

How you relate to money often reflects how you relate to yourself. Undercharging mirrors undervaluing. Refusing help mirrors difficulty receiving. The outer and inner are always in conversation.

✨

The Journey

6 portals. A daily practice. Gentle, evidence-based prompts that move you from scarcity to safety, from safety to worthiness, from worthiness to receiving. You are allowed to have more.

Week 1 · Day 1
Step 1 of 7 · Noticing
What money moment activated you today?
Checking your bank balance, a bill arriving, pricing something, someone talking about money, a moment of wanting something you felt you couldn't have...
Step 2 of 7 · The Story
What did you tell yourself in that moment?
The voice that spoke up — about you, about money, about what is possible for you...
Step 3 of 7 · Body
Where did you feel it in your body?
Our bodies hold our money stories long before our minds can name them. Select what you noticed, then describe the sensation.
Chest tightness
Throat constricting
Stomach drop
Shoulders tensing
Holding my breath
Heat or flushing
Going numb
Feeling small
Step 4 of 7 · Roots
Where did you first learn this about money?
Let a memory come gently — it might be something said, something witnessed, something felt. You don't have to force it.
Step 5 of 7 · Protection
What was this belief trying to protect you from?
Every money belief made sense when it was formed. What threat did it guard against? Rejection, conflict, loss, standing out, being abandoned?
Step 6 of 7 · Compassion
What does your wiser, loving self want to say?
Speak to her with the warmth you would offer a dear friend. She did her best with what she knew.
Step 7 of 7 · One New Truth
What new belief about money feels more true today?
Not toxic positivity — a genuinely gentler truth you can reach. Even just slightly softer counts.
